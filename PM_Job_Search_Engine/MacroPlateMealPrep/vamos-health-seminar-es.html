  • Every hormone in your body is built from fat Testosterone, estrogen, cortisol — all require dietary fat
  • Your brain is 60% fat — it needs to be fed Low-fat diets linked to brain fog, mood swings, depression
  • Fat absorbs vitamins A, D, E, K Remove fat = block key nutrients
  • The real culprit: trans fats, not natural fats Avoid: partially hydrogenated oils

🥩 Diapositiva 7 de 15 · Proteina

Proteina: la Consistencia Es Clave

  • Repairs muscle, supports metabolism, keeps you full The most satiating macronutrient per calorie
  • Spread protein across all 3 meals Your body absorbs ~30–40g at a time effectively
  • Most people skip protein at breakfast This sets up energy crashes and cravings by noon
  • Plant sources count — you don't need meat every meal Frijoles, lentils, tofu, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese

DAILY PROTEIN TARGETS

Sedentary adults 0.8g/kg
Active adults 1.2–1.6g/kg
Weight loss / muscle retention 1.6–2.2g/kg
