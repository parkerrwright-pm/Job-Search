Detailed example: ICHRA funding details modernization

- Worked on FNDT-5229, Funding MFE - Funding Details - ICHRA Updates.
- Problem addressed: ICHRA campaigns did not use allocation schedules, but the funding experience depended on allocation-based logic, causing some participants to appear as if they had no funding.
- Product goal: make Funding Details reflect ICHRA subsidy information accurately and align behavior with the FIT Funding Snapshot reference experience.
- Key outcomes:
  - ICHRA participant profiles and funding tiles were shown instead of misleading "No funding found" states.
  - Funding details used projected subsidy information rather than allocation schedule data.
  - The QUALIFIED row was removed for ICHRA where it created confusion.
  - REQUIREMENTS behavior was simplified to a more-info pattern with fixed plain-language explanation.
  - Non-ICHRA behavior remained unchanged, reducing regression risk.
- Business and user impact:
  - Improved clarity for contact center and operations teams.
  - Better representation of ICHRA funding behavior.
  - Reduced need to switch tools or guess at participant funding context.
